Our country Nepal

Nepal, officially the Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal, is a landlocked country
in South Asia. It has an area of 147,181 square km. Kathmandu is its capital city.
Nepal’s official language is Nepali and its official currency is Nepalese Rupee
(NPR). It has two bordering countries that are India and China. Nepal is a country
of breathtaking beauty and diversity of cultures like no other place on earth. Truly
“the roof of the world”, here are 71 interesting facts about Nepal covering its
history, culture, people, economy, the Himalayas, earthquakes, poverty,
Kathmandu, flora and fauna and more.
